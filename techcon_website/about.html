Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate innovators looking to share breakthroughs in software engineering, AI, and emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees from more than 50 countries.

From humble beginnings, TechCon has evolved into a hub of collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and groundbreaking announcements that shape the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to empower individuals and organizations by connecting them with cutting-edge innovations, inspiring talks, and transformative ideas. Our mission is to create an environment where creativity and technology can thrive together.

Through hands-on workshops, keynote speeches, and interactive exhibitions, TechCon fosters a strong community centered around progress, inclusion, and technological advancement.
